About Us

Welcome to CAKE HEAVEN SHOP, your destination for delicious, handcrafted cakes and baked goods. We are passionate about creating beautiful, mouth-watering treats for every occasion, from birthdays and weddings to corporate events or just a little something sweet for yourself.

Our journey began in 2015 with a mission to bring joy to people through our baked creations. Every cake we make is a labor of love, carefully crafted using the finest ingredients. Whether you're craving a classic vanilla sponge or a decadent chocolate cake, we have something for everyone.
